ITINERARY

Day Destination Activities
Day 1 Ooty On the first day, early morning set to Ooty to enjoy the exotic climate. Upon your arrival in Ooty, get into the hotel and relax for sometime. Later, start for local sightseeing tour to Rose garden which is famous in Ooty, Botanical garden in Udhagamandalam, The Gardens include approximately a thousand species, mutually exotic and indigenous, of shrubs, trees, plants, ferns, herbal and bonsai plants. The center of attraction is the fossilized tree trunk predictable to be 20 million years old. Ooty Lake and wax museum which is said to be the prominent places amidst the tourist spots in Ooty. Enjoy the night stay at hotel with the amazing cool climate.
Day 2 Ooty Take your breakfast in the hotel. Proceed to Mudumalai wild life sanctuary, the famous Pykara falls and later visit 6th mile and 7th mile where you can enjoy a hourse ride and play games. Continue to shooting point, and Fine forest. At the evening, set to home town by carrying sweet memories of Ooty.
