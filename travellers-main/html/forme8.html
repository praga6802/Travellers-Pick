ITINERARY

Day Destination Activities
Day 1 Coimbatore - Delhi Upon arrival at Delhi Airport, you will be meet by our tour representatives and taken to hotel. Get into the hotel and relax for sometime. Get ready for a Delhi local sightseeing tour to visit laces like India Gate and Shopping at Karol Bagh. Enjoy the rest of the day for relaxation. Get back to the hotel for overnight stay.
Day 2 Delhi - Amritsar After morning breakfast, visit Delhi sightseeing places such as Birla Mandir, Rajghat, Lotus temple. Evening, proceed to Amritsar (470 kilomters 11 hrs), Indira Gandhi Memorial. Spend your overnight journey peacefully.
Day 3 Amristar On arrival at Amritsar, proceed for Amritsar local sightseeing places such as Golden temple and so on. After lunch, proceed to Jallian vala bagh and Indo-Pak Wagha border. Move back to Amritsar hotel for overnight stay.
Day 4 Amrister - Shimla On this day early morning, drive to Shimla (300 kilometers 07 ½ hrs), Reach at Shimla set to hotel. In the evening, visit the Shimla Mall Road. Spend your night stay at Shimla hotel.
Day 5 Shimla After morning breakfast Shimla sightseeing places Himalayan zoo, Kufri -Winter sports capital, In the Evening visit Mall Road. Return back to Shimla hotel for overnight stay.
Day 6 Shimla - Kullu - Manali After a healthy breakfast, proceed to Manali (260 kilometers 9 hrs.) pass by Sutlej River, Beas River, and Kullu valley. Visit Shawl factory at Kullu. Reach Manali in the evening, Enjoy your night stay at Manali hotel.
Day 7 Manali - Rohtang pass - Manali Today, enjoy unimaginable drive towards Rohtang Pass Snow point at an altitude of 3,980 meters and enjoy in snow. Return for overnight stay to Manali hotel.
Day 8 Manali - Chandigarh After a delicious breakfast, visit the ancient temples like Vashishtha Temple and Hidimba Temple, Later, continue to Chandigarh (290 kilometers 08 hrs), Reach at Chandigarh (Chandigarh the most significant city of Independent India), After lunch, enjoy boat ride in Sukhna Lake and evening for free shopping, Overnight stay at Chandigarh hotel.
Day 9 Chandigarh - Delhi After breakfast, visit the Chandigarh Local sightseeing places Rock Gardens, Pinjore Gardens, and Rose Gardens. After lunch continue to Delhi (260 kilometers 06 hrs). Reach at Delhi and overnight stay at Delhi hotel.
Day 10 Delhi - Coimbatore After morning breakfast, drive for shopping and move to Delhi airport. Tour concludes with the sweet memories of North India.
