ITINERARY

Day Destination Activities
Day 1 Tirupathi On arrival at Tirupati, you will be greeted by our tour representatives and taken to Pamban Bridge at Rameshwaram. Later, get into the hotel in Tirupati and relax for some time. In the evening, get ready for an optional sightseeing tour to Tirupati. Move back to the hotel in Tirupati for overnight stay.
Day 2 Tirupati- Bangalore ( 250 Kms) Today after breakfast, continue for the Tirupathi touring which incorporates the Sri Padmavati Samovar Temple, Tiruchanoor, Sri Kalyana Venkateshwaraswami Temple, Sri Kodandaramaswami Temple, Sri Kapileshwaraswami Temple, Sri Govindarajaswami Temple, Shrinivasa Mangapuram. By afternoon, proceed to Bangalore. Spend your overnight stay at the hotel in Bangalore.
Day 3 Bangalore - Mysore ( 150 Kms) After morning breakfast continue on a touring excursion of Bangalore, Visit the Chennai FortCity of Lakes, Gardens and Parks. Visit the Lal Bagh, Vidhana Soudha, Bangalore Fort, Cubbon Park, Tippu Siltan's Summer Palace, The Mayo Hall, Attara Kacheri, Bangalore Palace, and Bull Temple etc. Later by evening, proceed to Mysore. Return back to the hotel in Mysore for overnight stay.
Day 4 Mysore - Ooty ( 130 Kms) After morning breakfast, continue for a tour to Mysore, The Brindavan arrangements, Jagmohan Palace, Lalitha Mahal, the Chamundi Hills. Visit the Mysore Palace and St. Philomena's Church. Later, continue to Ooty. Spend your overnight stay at the hotel in Ooty.
Day 5 Ooty Following breakfast, continue on a touring day out to Ooty. Visit the beauty of Rose Sightseeing at Chennai garden, The Botanical arrangements, Ooty Lake, Stone house, Toda Huts, The Mountain Railway, St. Stephens Church, Wax world and more. Proceed to the hotel in Ooty for overnight stay.
Day 6 Ooty - Madurai ( 290 Kms) Following breakfast, move on to Madurai. On the way, visit the Dolphins rock and Coonoor highlights. On arrival at Madurai, worship the Madurai Meenakshi Temple. Get back to the hotel room for night stay.
Day 7 Madurai Today after morning breakfast, proceed for a sightseeing tour to Madurai. Visit the Gandhi Museum, Tirumalai Naykkar Palace, Vandiyur Mariamman Teppakulam, Rajaji Park etc. Spend your overnight stay at the hotel in Madurai.
Day 8 Madurai - Rameswaram ( 180 Kms) Today after breakfast, proceed to Rameswaram. Register with the hotel and chill out the sightseeing of Rameshwaram for a while before you go out for touring to various Beaches, the Pamban Bridge, The Agraharams and Temples. Get back to the hotel in Rameswaram for overnight stay.
Day 9 Rameswaram - Kanyakumari ( 290 Kms) After breakfast, continue to Kanyakumari. Visit the Vivekananda Rock, Gandhi Memorial, Dhyana Mandapam, Valluvar Statue, and the Sunrise and Sunset Beaches. Enjoy your overnight stay at the hotel in Kanyakumari.
Day 10 Departure On the final day of your trip after breakfast, proceed to Coimbatore for return journey.
