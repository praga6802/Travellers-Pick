ITINERARY

Day Destination Activities
Day 1 Pune - Shirdi | Arrival Meet our representative at the pre-decided pick-up location in Pune and get transferred to Shirdi. After reaching Shirdi, check in to the hotel and after freshening up, proceed ahead to visit the sacred Shani Shingnapur Temple, known as "jagrut devasthan”,alive temple of Lord Shani to seek blessings. In the evening, spend some time exploring the town. Return back to the hotel and enjoy comfortable overnight stay an dinner in Shirdi.
Day 2 Shirdi | Visit to Sansthan Temple Wake up int morning, have a hearty breakfast and get ready for a temple tour of Shirdi. Visit the Sansthan Temple also known as the Saibaba Temple and seek blessings from Shree Sai Baba. Visit Dwarkamai which is the heart of Shirdi as Sai Baba spent his last few days over here. You can also visit Chavdi, Khandoba Temple, Maruti Temple, Nand deep, Sai Museum as all are situated in the same complex. In th evening, return back to the hotel, enjoy your dinner and then retire for the night.
Day 3 Shirdi to Nashik | Drive to the Wine City After having breakfast, complete the check out formalities and start your journey to Nashik, which will take 3 hours to reach. On your way, you will witness the beautiful view of the lush green valley. On arrival at Nashik, complete the check in formalities and head out to visit Trimbakeshwar Shiva Temple, located in the Trimbak town. Visit Mukthidham, a temple made from white marble, Panchavati, the home of five ancient Banyan Trees, Kalaram Temple, Goraram Temple, Sita Gufa and Kapaleeshwar Temple. You can also visit Sula Vineyards, the biggest winemaker in India spread over 160 acres of area in Nashik. Reach back to the hotel, enjoy your delicious dinner and an overnight stay in Nashik..
Day 4 Nashik - Pune | Bid Farewell Wake in the morning, have a toothsome breakfast, complete the checkout formalities and travel back to Pune. Conclude the tour with sweet memories on reaching Pune.
