ITINERARY

Day Destination Activities
Day 1 Arrival Njp Railway / (Ixb) Bagdogra Airport - Gangtok Upon your arrival at NJP Railway Station or (IXB) Bagdogra Airport, our tour representatives will welcome and help to transfer to the vehicle to Gangtok (5500 Feet /1677 Mts, 130 Kilometers / 04 to 05 Hrs). Sign into the hotel and enjoy your over night stay at the hotel.
Day 2 Gangtok Take your breakfast in the morning. Get ready for a pleasure trip to Baba Mandir & Tshangu Lake - located at a height of 12400Ft / 3780 Mts / 43Kms in 3Hrs. The cool water of the Lake is absolutely adjusted with the picturesque beauty around. Return back to Gangtok and spend your overnight stay at the hotel. In case of Land slide or any problem, the Tshangu Lake will be closed. We will provide some other places of interest to visit.
Day 3 Gangtok - Kalimpong Proceed for a half day touring covering Jhakri Water Falls (04 hrs) along with Research Institute of Tibetology, Flower Show, Directorate of Handicraft & Handloom (Closed on Sunday), Droul Chorthen. In the evening, switch over to Kalimpong (3937 Ft/ 1200 Mts, 85 Kms/ 03 to 04 Hrs), Sign in & over night stay at hotel.
Day 4 Kalimpong - Darjeeling After morning breakfast visit Dello slopes, Flower Nurseries, Dr. Graham's Home, Jang-Dong-Palriffo Brang and Durbin Dara Hills. Drive to Darjeeling (7380 Ft/ 2250 Mts, 70 Kms/ 03 to 04 Hrs), night at the hotel.
Day 5 Darjeeling Early morning, visit the Tiger Hills (8364 Ft/ 2550 Mts at around 4 AM) to view the dawn over Kanchendzonga Peak (subject to clear climate). Ensure to visit Ghoom Monastery, Batasia Loop. After breakfast, get ready for a half-day touring to Peace pagoda, Padmaja Naidu Himalayan Zoological Park , Japanese Temple, & Himalayan Mountaineering Institute (Closed on Thursday), Darjeeling Ropeway Ride (Ticket cost excluded & will be approx INR 180.00 for every individual) Tenzing and Gombu Rock, Tibetan Refugee Self-Help Center (Closed on Sunday) & surrounding perspective of Tea Estate. Enjoy your overnight stay at the hotel.
Day 6 Darjeeling After morning breakfast, travel to Mirik (5800 Ft/ 1768 Mts/ 50 Kms/ 02 Hrs one way). A full day trip of Pashupati Nagar (Indo-Nepal outskirt) for shopping & Mirik Lake (Horse riding & Boat is accessible around the lake on immediate payment principle). Get back to Darjeeling and enjoy your overnight stay at the hotel.
Day 7 Darjeeling - Njp Railway Station / (Ixb) Bagdogra Airport After morning breakfast, continue to NJP Railway Station / (IXB) Bagdogra Airport (100 Kms / 03 to 04 Hrs), for your onward journey.
