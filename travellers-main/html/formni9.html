ITINERARY

Day Destination Activities
Day 1 Delhi - Nainital (310kms / 8hrs) After your arrival at Airport/ Railway Station, our team representatives will take you to a drive to Nainital. You will be having your lunch on the way. On arrival, get into the hotel and relax for sometime. In the evening, move for a boat ride at Naini Lake (By personal) and enjoy great pleasure
Day 2 Nainital (Local Sight Seeing) Take your breakfast in the morning. Get ready for a lake tour to Sat Tal, Bhim Tal, Naukuchia Tal. Drive back to hotel and enjoy a sumptuous lunch. After lunch, you can try a boat ride at Naini Lake & Rope way (At their own). In the evening, just move to Mall Road and explore great fun.
Day 3 Nanital - Corbett National Park (70 km/ 2hrs ) After a quick breakfast, start from Nanital to Corbett National Park and visit the Corbett Museum, Corbett fall. Take a break for lunch. Proceed to the Corbett National Park. Located in the foothills of Himalayas, Corbett National Park is a popular park in the world. It is famous for its wild inhabitants such as Leopards, Tigers. Move back to the hotel for dinner.
Day 4 Corbett to Haridwar (265 km/7hr ) Take a quick breakfast in the morning. Move out from the hotel and proceed to Haridwar. After your arrival, get into the hotel and refresh yourself. Visit the Ganga Ji ki Aarti at Har ki Pori. Get back to the hotel for dinner.
Day 5 Haridwar to Mussoorie via Rishikesh ( 85kms/ 4hr) After an early breakfast, move from the hotel and depart to Mussoori. On the way, visit Bharat Mata Mandir, Laxman Zula, Swrag Ashram. After your arrival, get into the hotel and refresh yourself. Ensure to get into the Mall Road and Rope way. Move back to the hotel for dinner.
Day 6 Mussoorie Take your breakfast in the morning. Our tour representatives will take you to Dhanolty, Mussoorie Lake and Mall Road. Enjoy a great dinner at the hotel. O/N/S.
Day 7 Mussoorie - Delhi (285 kms 7 hrs) After breakfast, move from the hotel and proceed to Delhi. On the way, stop at Dehradoon. On arrival at Delhi, move to the airport and wait for your departure.
